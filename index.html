About


MASTER DESIGNER V1.0, presented by the Media Unit of SLTC, was a 4-day workshop series on Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign graphic design tools.


Its second incarnation, “MASTER DESIGNER V2.0,” is an all-island competition organized by the Media Unit of SLTC to evaluate the most talented people in the field of graphic design. The goal of this competition is not only to choose the best of the best but also to create a platform that will use AI technology to expand the boundaries of graphic design. The competition in MASTER DESIGNER V2.0 will be held in two phases: an online competition and a physical competition. Also, they can compete in this competition individually or in teams of three members.



         



 



PHASES

PHASE 01

In the first phase, designs for one field selected from the given fields are submitted under the online mode. During the submission, the contestants should submit a suitable name for the creation and the contestants can submit the design individually or as a team of three members. 15 teams and 15 individual competitors will be selected for the final stage of the competition that is the physical competition round.

01

"Submit Your Creation, Name Your Vision, Inspire the Future"

PHASE 02

In the second phase (final phase) the two selected groups, 15 teams and 15 individual competitors, will measure their skills at a specific time. There will be one task for individual competitors and three tasks for group competitors. Among the designs created in this way, the greatest designs are evaluated.

GuideLines

Participants can compete individually or in teams of up to three members
Competition Phases The competition will be conducted in two phases: an online competition followed by a physical competition.
Online Submission Participants must submit their design for one selected field online, including a suitable name and a brief description of the design.
Physical Competition In the physical competition, individual contestants will complete one main task, and teams will complete three tasks within a specified time.
Bonus Tasks Participants can complete additional small tasks during the physical competition to earn bonus points.
